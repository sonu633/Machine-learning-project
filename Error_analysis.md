# 10 failure cases
.Human text  can carry hidden emotions

.System detects positive due to the word “Great,” but actual emotion is negative.

.Emotional expressions differ across cultures.

.Facial expression shows happiness, but text indicates sadness

.More “happy” samples than “anger” or “fear.”

.Leads to incorrect emotional inference

.Model simplifies complex emotions into basic categories.

.Emotions change over time

.Important signals like tone, pauses, or context are ignored.
