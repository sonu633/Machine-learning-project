# 10 failure cases
1.Human text  can carry hidden emotions

2.System detects positive due to the word “Great,” but actual emotion is negative.

3.Emotional expressions differ across cultures.

4.Facial expression shows happiness, but text indicates sadness

5.More “happy” samples than “anger” or “fear.”

6.Leads to incorrect emotional inference

7.Model simplifies complex emotions into basic categories.

8.Emotions change over time

9.Important signals like tone, pauses, or context are ignored.

10.Features selected may not truly represent emotion.


# insight
Systematic error: consistent pattern (e.g., always misclassifying sad as neutral)

.Random error : no clear pattern
 Insight: Focus on fixing systematic errors first (highest impact)
